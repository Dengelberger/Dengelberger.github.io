How to make a Ham and Cheese sandwich:


  1. Get a small pan ready and heat it.
  2. Assemble the ham and cheese between two slices of bread.
  3. Spread the outside of the bread with butter.
  4. Put the sandwich in the pan and toast on each side for 1 minute.
  5. Take the sandwich out of the pan, cut into pieces, and enjoy!
