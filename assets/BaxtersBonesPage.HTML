How to make doggo treats:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Mix oatmeal, peanut butter, whole wheat flour, eggs, honey, chicken stock, and bacon.
  3. Roll out and cut out dog-bone shape cookies.
  4. Place on a parchment-lined cookie sheet and bake for 8 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven, cool on wire rack, and enjoy!
